days until the vote

Tell the EU Parliament to curb geoblocking on April 24

The EU is taking action – but they want to exclude online videos from the law against geoblocking.

The European Parliament can make it better. They will vote on whether to include videos in the law on Monday, April 24, 2017.

Contact your MEP today and tell them:

Support Geoblocking Amendment 281 – to limit geoblocking for videos, too

Contacting your MEP (or their assistants) is easy and takes just 2 minutes. It is their job to represent you, so don't be afraid to tell them that you reject digital borders in Europe and expect them to vote against geoblocking in all cases. You don't need expert knowledge – just be polite. You can mention some of the following arguments:
